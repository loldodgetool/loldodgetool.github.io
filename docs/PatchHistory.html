Known issues


  • TLDR: Champion counters are not accurate due to id bug.
    Details:
    With the introduction of new champions, the model got some of the ids mixed up, so when you say champion A the model thinks champion B. So what happens is that it may think you are good at champion B but in reality you are good at champion A, but the good news is that picking champion A in champion select will also make it think you are talking about champion A, "the champion you are good at". So the prediction won't have counters and teamcomp quality. For lower elo (Below D1) this shouldn't affect the prediction that much.
